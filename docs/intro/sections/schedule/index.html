    Schedule #

    The course consists of 60 hours of lectures and 30 hours of labs.

    Lectures #

    By default, lectures are scheduled:

    • Monday 13:30 - 15:30
    • Tuesday 08:30 - 10:30
    • Wednesday 08:30 - 10:30

    All students attend the same lectures.

    Lectures can be followed via Teams (e.g. for students sitting at the back of the class), by joining the corresponding meeting (in the “Lectures” channel of this course’s Team).

    Labs #

    By default, labs are scheduled:

    • Monday 15:30 - 17:30
    • Wednesday 13:30 - 15:30

    Students are split in two groups (to be determined) for the labs, with identical content. Students who collaborate on the same group project are expected to attend the same lab.

    Most labs will be dedicated to the assignments for this course. Two labs will be dedicated to a presentation and discussion of the advancement of the group projects.

    Timetable #

    Some weeks will see fewer lectures/labs, and schedules or room numbers may vary from one week to the other. So make sure to check the timetable.
